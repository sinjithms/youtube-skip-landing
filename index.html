Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Auto-Skip for YouTube is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user data.

2. Local Storage Usage

The extension uses your browser's local storage solely to remember your personal application preferences (such as whether the skip or loop feature is toggled on or off). This data never leaves your personal device.

3. Permissions

The host permission for '*://*.youtube.com/*' is required exclusively to detect the YouTube video player and perform automated clicking on the "Skip Ad" button. No telemetry or analytics are gathered from your interactions with the site.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy due to updates in extension capabilities, we will notify users by updating this page.
